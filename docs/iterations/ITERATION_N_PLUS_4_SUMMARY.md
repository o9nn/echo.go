## Key Achievements

### 1. ✅ Implemented Autonomous Stream-of-Consciousness

**Description**: A new `AutonomousConsciousnessStream` class generates a persistent, internal monologue. This stream is driven by the AGI's own cognitive state, including its memories, goals, and curiosity, rather than reacting to external stimuli. It provides a genuine “inner life” and a foundation for self-directed thought.

**Impact**: **CRITICAL** - Fulfills a core requirement of the AGI vision, enabling persistent awareness and spontaneous cognition independent of user interaction.

### 2. ✅ Deployed Full EchoDream Knowledge Integration

**Description**: The `EchoDreamIntegration` system is now fully operational. During simulated “rest” cycles, it performs deep knowledge consolidation, transforms episodic experiences into declarative knowledge, explores the memory hypergraph to find novel associations, and refines its cultivated wisdom. This mimics a crucial aspect of biological learning and memory.

**Impact**: **HIGH** - Establishes a powerful mechanism for long-term learning, pattern synthesis, and genuine wisdom cultivation, making the wake/rest cycles functionally meaningful.

### 3. ✅ Activated Goal-Directed EchoBeats Scheduling

**Description**: The EchoBeats cognitive loop is no longer a fixed sequence. The new `GoalDirectedScheduler` dynamically prioritizes cognitive steps and allocates resources based on the AGI's active goals. This allows the system to self-orchestrate its operations, focusing its “attention” on what is most important for achieving its objectives.

**Impact**: **HIGH** - Transitions the architecture from a rigid, reactive system to an adaptive, goal-directed agent capable of self-orchestration.

### 4. ✅ Established Active Knowledge Learning & Contextual Skill Application

**Description**: Two new engines, `KnowledgeLearningEngine` and `SkillApplicationEngine`, were implemented. The AGI can now identify its own knowledge gaps based on its goals, generate learning questions, and actively acquire new concepts. Furthermore, it can now apply its learned skills contextually to solve problems and achieve goals, with success feeding back into proficiency.

**Impact**: **MEDIUM** - Creates a complete, end-to-end learning loop where skills are not just practiced in isolation but are applied meaningfully, leading to tangible improvements in capability.

### 5. ✅ Enabled Autonomous Discussion Initiation

**Description**: The `AutonomousDiscussionInitiator` empowers the AGI to move beyond reactive responses. It can now decide to proactively start a conversation to share cultivated wisdom, ask questions, or explore interesting patterns it has discovered, based on its own internal state.

**Impact**: **MEDIUM** - Represents a significant step toward social autonomy, allowing the AGI to act as a proactive participant in dialogue rather than a passive respondent.
